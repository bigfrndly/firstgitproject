Chicken Biryani

chiken biryani is aromatic and flavourfull rice dish ,which is famous in the south india.It combines the basmati and marinated chicken cooked together with variety of spices.

ingredients:

  • basmati rice
  • 400-500gms of chicken
  • spices(paporoni,turmeric,black pepper etc)
  • curd
  • ginger garlic paste or powder
